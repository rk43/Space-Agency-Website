About IAST

Indian Academy of Space Technology (IAST), situated at Darbhanga ,Bihar .IAST functions as an autonomous body under the Department of Space, Government of India. The idea of such an institute was mooted keeping in mind the need for high quality manpower for the Indian Space Research Organization, one of worldâ€™s leading scientific organizations engaged in space research and space applications. The institute is the first of its kind in the country, to offer high quality education at the undergraduate, graduate, doctoral and post-doctoral levels on areas with special focus to space sciences, space technology and space applications.

The institute provides for the publication charges of peer reviewed journal papers that are published by faculty, research scholars and students of the institute. IAST also encourages its faculty to closely collaborate with scientists/engineers from ISRO/DoS through joint research projects funded by the institute. IAST also encourages its faculty to write research proposals for funding from external sources. IAST also has a number of international collaborations, both as MOU at the level of institute and faculty-to-faculty collaboration.
